Program Overview

Our Early Childhood program is designed to nurture young learners, fostering growth and curiosity in a safe and stimulating environment. Through a balanced curriculum, we aim to provide a solid foundation for lifelong learning and development.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Original and photocopy of birth certificate
  • Updated immunization records
  • 2 recent passport-sized photos
  • Other requirements as specified by the school

Enrollment Process SY 2024-2025

  1. Submit the completed application form along with the required documents.
  2. Attend a scheduled orientation session for parents and child assessment.
  3. Pay the necessary fees to confirm enrollment.
